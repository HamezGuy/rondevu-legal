Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2024
Last Updated: December 15, 2024

This Privacy Policy applies to both Rondevu (consumer app) and RondevuManagement (business management app), collectively referred to as "Rondevu Apps" or "our Services," operated by Rondevu Events ("we," "us," or "our").

Welcome to Rondevu! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

For Rondevu (Consumer App):
  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, profile photo
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Event Preferences: Interests, saved events, RSVP status, followed venues/artists
  • Location Data: Current location (with permission) to show nearby events
  • Communications: Messages sent through the app, support inquiries
  • User Content: Reviews, ratings, photos uploaded to events
For RondevuManagement (Business App):
  • Business Account Information: Business name, email, phone, address, tax ID
  • Financial Information: Bank account details for payouts (via Stripe Connect)
  • Event Management Data: Event details, ticket sales, attendee lists
  • Venue/Artist Profile: Business hours, menus, specials, performance schedules
  • Staff Information: Employee/team member accounts and permissions
  • Analytics Data: Sales reports, customer demographics, engagement metrics

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, pages viewed, actions taken, timestamps
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, app version, crash reports
  • Location Information: Precise location (with permission), approximate location from IP
  • Push Notification Tokens: To send event updates and reminders

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media: If you connect social accounts, we receive basic profile information
  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmations from Stripe
  • Analytics Providers: Aggregated usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide Our Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process ticket purchases and payments
  • Send booking confirmations and QR codes
  • Enable messaging between users and businesses
  • Provide customer support
  • Display personalized event recommendations
  • Facilitate event check-ins via QR code scanning

2.2 To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Conduct research and development
  • Test new features and functionality
  • Monitor and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Ensure platform security and integrity

2.3 To Communicate With You

  • Send event reminders and updates
  • Notify about changes to events you're attending
  • Provide customer support responses
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Deliver important service announcements

2.4 Legal and Safety Purposes

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to legal requests and prevent harm
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect rights, property, and safety

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who help us operate:
    • Stripe for payment processing (PCI compliant)
    • Firebase for authentication and analytics
    • Google Maps for location services
    • Cloud storage providers for media files
  • Event Organizers: Basic attendee information for events you purchase tickets to
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers or acquisitions
  • With Consent: When you explicitly agree to sharing

4. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Secure Storage: Personal information is stored in encrypted databases
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication available for business accounts
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions limit data access
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates applied promptly

4.2 Payment Security

  • We never store credit card numbers directly
  • All payments processed through PCI-compliant Stripe
  • Tokenization used for recurring payments
  • Fraud detection and prevention measures in place

4.3 Incident Response

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Provide details about what information was compromised
  • Offer guidance on protective measures
  • Cooperate with regulatory authorities

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Port: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restrict: Limit how we use your information
  • Object: Opt-out of certain data processing

5.2 Communication Preferences

  • Manage push notification settings in app settings
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails via link in emails
  • Control in-app notification preferences
  • Opt-out of promotional communications

5.3 Location Services

  • Disable location services in device settings
  • Use app without precise location (limited functionality)
  • Clear location history from your profile

5.4 Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  • Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app
  • Or email us at privacy@rondevuevents.com
  • We will delete your data within 30 days
  • Some information may be retained for legal compliance

6. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account, and certain features (such as ticket purchases for 21+ events) require users to be 21 or older.

If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe we have information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at privacy@rondevuevents.com.

7. International Data Transfers

Our Services are operated in the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our Services, you consent to this transfer, processing, and storage of your information in the United States.

8. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rondevuevents.com or call 1-888-RONDEVU.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Information: Retained while account is active
  • Transaction Records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Communications: 2 years or as required by law
  • Analytics Data: 2 years in aggregated form
  • Deleted Account Data: Removed within 30 days (except legally required data)

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification
  • Emailing you if changes are significant

Your continued use of our Services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
