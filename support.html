Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Download the Rondevu app from the App Store, tap "Sign Up" and follow the prompts to create your account using your email or phone number.
How do I find events near me?
Open the app and allow location access when prompted. The home screen will automatically show events happening near your current location. You can also use the search and filter features to find specific types of events.
How do I purchase tickets?
Find an event you're interested in, tap on it to view details, then tap "Get Tickets". Select your ticket type and quantity, then complete the secure checkout process.
How do I delete my account?
Go to your profile, tap the settings menu (three dots), and select "Delete Account". Follow the confirmation prompts. Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and Apple Pay through our secure payment processor, Stripe.
How do I contact an event organizer?
On the event details page, you can find the organizer's profile. Tap on it to view their contact information or send them a message through the app.

Technical Support

Experiencing technical issues? Try these steps:

  • Update to the latest version of the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app
  • Clear app cache (iOS: offload app in Settings)
  • If issues persist, contact us at jgui@rondevuevents.com
